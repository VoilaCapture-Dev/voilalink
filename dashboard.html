How to restore your backup:
  1. Click Restore from Backup above
  2. Select your voilalink-backup-YYYY-MM-DD.json file
  3. Confirm the prompt — your settings will be overwritten with the backup
  4. The page will reload automatically with all your data restored
⚠ Restoring a backup will overwrite your current links and settings. This cannot be undone. Always download a fresh backup first.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I change my username?
Go to Settings → update your username field → Save
Will my links work if I'm on the free plan?
Yes, all links are always live on your bio page regardless of plan
What happens when my trial ends?
Pro features lock but your links and profile stay live. You can upgrade at any time.
Can I use VoilaLink on mobile?
Yes, the dashboard has a mobile view and your bio page is fully responsive
How do I add my bio page to my Instagram?
Copy your voilalink.com/username link from the top of the dashboard and paste it in your Instagram bio
